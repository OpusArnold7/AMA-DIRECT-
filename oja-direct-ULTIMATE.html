What exactly is Oja-Direct?
Think of it like a buying club. Instead of one person buying a bag of rice from the market, 20–50 neighbors buy together. Because the order is big, the farmer sells cheaper. You are the one who organizes this — and you take a 10% fee for doing so.
💡 Analogy: You're like the organizer of a "bulk buy trip" to a warehouse. Everyone pays you their share, you buy in bulk and earn a cut.
💰
How do I actually earn money?
Every time a customer pays ₦60,000 for rice, ₦6,000 automatically goes to your wallet via Paystack. You never touch the food, never drive a truck. The platform handles payment, the wholesaler handles goods, the captain handles delivery. You get paid automatically.
💡 Example: 10 orders in one day = ₦60,000 in your pocket. 100 orders/week = ₦600,000/week.
🏘️
What is a "State Captain"?
A Captain is a local person (like an estate secretary or cooperative leader) who finds buyers in their neighborhood. They share the platform link on WhatsApp, collect orders, and coordinate Saturday delivery. They earn 5% per order. You earn 10%. They do the local work. You built the system.
💡 Think of Captains like your local sales agents — they bring customers, you split the profit.
📦
Do I need to store food or own a truck?
No. You own none of the goods. You never touch the food. The wholesaler owns and delivers the goods. You own the platform — the website, the payment flow, the customer relationships. You make money by connecting people, not by holding inventory.
💡 You're like an Airbnb host who owns the app — not the houses. The landlords (wholesalers) do the physical work.
📱
How do customers find and pay?
Customers visit this website, click their state, find their neighborhood group, enter their details, and pay via Paystack (card, bank transfer, OPay, USSD). Everything is online. No cash handling, no manual receipts. Paystack sends them a receipt automatically and splits the money to everyone involved.
💡 Your job is just to bring people to the website. Once they're here, the platform does the rest.
🎯
How long before I earn my first ₦100,000?
If you get 2 State Captains and each has 30 customers ordering weekly, that's 60 orders × ₦6,000 = ₦360,000 profit per week. Your first ₦100k comes within the first week of real orders. The 100-Day Plan on the Growth page shows you exactly how to get there.
💡 Focus on Week 1: Get ONE Captain. Help them get 20 buyers. That's ₦120,000 your first week.

💬 WhatsApp Cloud API — The Upgrade You Must Make at Scale
At 1,000 customers/week, a regular phone number will get banned by Meta for high message volume. Before you hit 500 orders, upgrade to the WhatsApp Cloud API (free via Meta for Business). This runs a proper AI bot — not a phone.
1
Register at business.facebook.com — Create a Meta Business Account and verify your business phone number as a WhatsApp Business API number.
2
Build with n8n or Make.com (free tier) — Set up automation flows: "User texts state name → bot replies with their state's open group buy link + current price." No coding required.
3
Add Smart Regional Routing — When a user in Enugu texts, bot automatically checks South East hub progress, not Lagos. One bot handles all 37 states automatically.
4
Paystack Webhook Integration — The moment a payment is confirmed, the bot auto-sends a receipt + delivery schedule to the customer. Zero manual work from you.
🌡️ Phase 3: Cold Chain Integration — The Billion-Naira Unlock
